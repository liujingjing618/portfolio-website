Overview

“Hello World” is a vocabulary learning app that I created this mobile concept for my UX fundamental course.

It provides short sessions that could fit into user’s busy schedule to learn practical everyday conversations. It also contains smart useful tools that can make user’s foreign trip easier.

Research

I conducted competitive analysis on 3 most popular vocabulary learning apps on the market: Duolingo, Babbel and Freerice.

I also interviewed a few potential users in different age groups then come up with the persona – Charlie Douglas.

Problem

Charlie is always on the go, either commuting between home and work, or going abroad for business trip or vacation. He needs a way to learn and practice vocabulary that fits into his busy schedule, also help him to overcome language barrier when he travels.

Solution

We believe by creating an interactive vocabulary app with gamification features that generates short sessions of practice based on users’level and interests, also includes some useful tools like voice translator, he can be motived on learning and maintaining a new vocabulary.

As soon as I get my initial prototype ready, I tested with 5 potential users by giving them 4 direct tasks and 4 scenario tasks.

The usability testing went smoothly with the participants. I collected quite a bit useful information and conducted “Usability Test Report” to improve my prototype.
